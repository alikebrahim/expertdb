Years of educational excellence.

The Education and Training Quality Authority (BQA)

plays a key role in shaping a high-quality, transparent, and accountable education and training system in Bahrain. We start with a simple question:
What does good education and training look like — and how can we help providers achieve it?
For over a decade, BQA has worked across all levels of education and training to:

  • Review institutions and programmes
  • Conduct national examinations
  • Administer the National Qualifications Framework (NQF)

Our independent work supports learners, parents, employers, and policymakers by providing clear insights into how education and training providers are performing — and how they can improve.
